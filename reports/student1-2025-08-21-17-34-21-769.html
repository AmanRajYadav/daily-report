What We Explored

English Grammar: Becoming a Question Master!

Today's adventure: Unlocking the power of questions to communicate clearly. We discovered how small 'power-up' words like 'can', 'has', and 'did' are the secret keys to asking about anything. We also explored how tenses are like time machines for our words, especially the 'present perfect continuous' (like 'has been shining'), which describes an action that started in the past and is still going on, like rain that hasn't stopped yet! The coolest part was seeing how just changing the word order turns a statement into a powerful question. This skill is essential for showing curiosity, solving problems, and connecting with people every single day.
