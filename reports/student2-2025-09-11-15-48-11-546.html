Your Mission

üéØ The Floating Compass Challenge

Your mission: Create a working compass with everyday items! Step 1: Find a sewing needle, a small magnet, a cork slice (or a small leaf), and a bowl of water. Step 2: Magnetize the needle by rubbing it on the magnet in one direction about 50 times. Step 3: Carefully place the cork/leaf in the water so it floats, then place the magnetized needle on top of it. Watch as it slowly spins to point North-South! Bonus challenge: Take it outside and see if you can identify North. How does its direction compare to where the sun is?

This activity is amazing because it brings science to life! You're making a tool that explorers have used for centuries and proving a fundamental law of physics in your own home. It helps your brain build stronger connections for understanding magnetism and Earth's invisible forces, plus you can show your family how to find their way without a phone!
