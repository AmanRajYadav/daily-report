Knowledge Unlocked

English Grammar: Becoming a Time-Traveling Storyteller with Verb Tenses!
Today's adventure was all about making our words powerful by choosing the right tense. Knowing different tenses is like having a time machine for your stories. We discovered that key words like 'since,' 'for,' and 'all morning' are secret clues that an action has been happening for a while, which tells us to use the Present Perfect Continuous (like 'It **has been snowing** since yesterday'). We also explored how a group, like 'The Public Health Department,' acts as a single unit, so it uses a singular verb like 'has'. The coolest part was seeing how you can mix tenses in a story to make it more alive! This skill will help you write more dynamic and interesting stories, whether for school or just for fun!
